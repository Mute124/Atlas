#define ATLAS_DIRECTIONAL_INPUT_COUNT   6

Defines the total number of directional inputs that the camera supports. The reason for only 6 directions is because that is the amount of directions that a 3D camera can move in. These are the 6 directions:

  • Forward
  • Backward
  • Right
  • Left
  • Up
  • Down
